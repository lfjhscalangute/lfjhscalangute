Vision & Objectives

Vision Statement

The Handmaids of Christ, animated and guided by the spirit of Msgr. Herculano Gonsalves, aim at integral formation and liberation of the human person. We strive to actively participate in the redemptive and reconciling work of Christ and Communion with the whole creation through the educational ministry.

In keeping with the missionary dimension of our charism, we seek to search for and execute God's will and His life giving plan for those we educate through:

i) A prefrential love for the poor and less privileged.
ii)The process of humanization in the context of today's brokenness.
iii)Respecting the human dignity of the poorest and working to promote freedom from all types of oppression.
iv) Faith which promotes Justice so that we live by God's call 'To act justly' at all times and at all levels and 'Love tenderly'-(Micah:6,8).
v)Complete confidence in God, in order to announce Jesus Christ to all our students, the conviction that God loves all without distinction of caste, religion or social status.
vi)Motivating our students to build a better world, and like our Founder, work for the promotion of the kingdom through an excercise of God's saving love expressed through compassionate justice.

Aims and Objectives


i.) The primary purpose of this School is to provide Catholic Education to Catholic students but all others are given admission and are given equal rights, and are expected to conform to the rules of the school.
ii) The aim of the School is to impart academic, physical, moral and spiritual education with particular emphasis on Catholic formation for Catholic students with due respect to the religious feelings and freedom of conscience of all students.
iii)It aims to train the children to learn intelligently and to help them to develop the qualities of co-operation and service so as to make them useful and worthy citizens of our Motherland.
