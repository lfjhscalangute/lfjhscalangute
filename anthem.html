School Anthem

1. Oh tender and gentle Little Flower
Loving Saint of our own modern day
With childlike fervor and simplicity
Conq'ed Jesus for all eternity.

Ch. Dear Saint teach us your little way
How to live for God and the other every day
Abundant showers of grace and love
May be poured on our School from above.

2. We promise to live the noble values
Keep the light burning in all its hues
Our motto "Duty First" will we abide
With the Lord march will we by His side.

3. To honour our Founder work shall we
Little Flower our heads bow to thee
We children our Saviour do we adore
Graces all that we need for us implore.
