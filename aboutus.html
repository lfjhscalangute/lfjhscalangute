OUR HISTORY

St. Britto High School active and dynamic learning environment provides them with a wholesome education experience.To ensure that not a single child is left out in this process, We have developed a multi-dimensional learning methodology for every child which helps to bring out their natural talent and give shape to their creative thinking. At school, We have impeccable infrastructure facilities for the pursuit of the teaching-learning activity.our science laboratories are well-equipped with modern scientific apparatus and equipment,to carry out experiments. Well-stacked library, projector& computer enabled classrooms, activity hall and sports facilities combine to form a perfect ground for unleashing the childs true potential

The St. Britto High School is committed to deliver the future citizens of of tomorrow with an astute intellect and an ingrained value system. We appreciate the unique,individual talents of children and follow an instructional strategy that suits their different learning styles .This multi-faceted learning teaching approach allows children to understand and imbibe lessons completely and apply them to real-life situations
