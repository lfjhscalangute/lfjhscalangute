Admission & Withdrawal

1. Every child seeking admission must be introduced in person by at least one parent or any one guardian who will be responsible to the school authorities for his/ her conduct.
2. A student who has attended any recognised school cannot be admitted without a Leaving Certificate from the school he/ she has last attended. If he/she comes from a school outside the state of Goa, the Leaving Certificate must be countersigned by the Education Officer of the State/District in which that school is situated or by anyone duly authorized.
Note: The prescribed procedure will be followed to admit students from other countries.
3. At the time of admission, an official Birth Certificate will be required if he/she joins school fresh from home, and the prescribed procedures will be applicable if the student seeks admission to any standard other than Std I. as per age.
4. All requests for the Leaving Certificate should be made in writing by the respective parents or guardians with due notice of at least one month.
5. The application for the Leaving Certificate should be submitted on 1st April. No Leaving Certificate will be issued after 30th April.
