Our History

Established in 1931 by the Late Msgr. Herculano Gonsalves is a School recognised and aided by the Government of Goa. It is managed by the "Educational Society of the Handmaids of Christ, Goa". The Society is a Religious based Minority Institution registered under Societies Registration order dated 31st March 1973 in the office of the Sub Registrar, Panaji under the Societies Registration Act 1860 under Registration No.09/R.S./39/73.

Aims & Objectives

i) The aim of the School is to impart academic, physical, moral and spiritual education for all students. iii)It aims to train the children to learn intelligently and to help them to develop the qualities of co-operation and service so as to make them useful and worthy citizens of our Motherland..

Information Teachnology

A SMART Board is an interactive whiteboard that works through touch detection. Students and staff can manipulate objects on the board such as images, shapes, videos, and written text.
